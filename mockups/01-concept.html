How it works under the hood

  • Push-to-start: the Headout backend launches the activity on the morning of the experience — the user never has to open the app.
  • Server-driven updates: a handful of pushes a day move it through the states (ready → leave → arrived → wrap-up). Low frequency, so no battery or rate-limit issues.
  • Lifespan: it's a day-of companion — active up to ~8 hours, lingering on the Lock Screen a few hours more. Confirmation and reminders stay in email & push.
  • The advice engine: experience metadata you already hold + venue rules + a live weather lookup, blended into at most three tips so the card never clutters.
