How it works under the hood

  • Push-to-start: the activity launches on the afternoon of the performance — the guest never opens the app.
  • Show-aware updates: a few server pushes move it through the night (tonight → leave → doors → interval → curtain), timed off the curtain and the 2h 30m runtime.
  • The interval beat: a moment only a live show has — a 20-minute heads-up, where the queues are, and what's coming in Act II.
  • Indoor logic: rain never panics the card — the venue's indoors, so weather only shapes the walk from the tube and the cloakroom nudge.
  • Lifespan: active for the evening and lingering briefly after; the booking confirmation stays in email & push.
